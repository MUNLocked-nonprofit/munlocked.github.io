About Us

We are a nonprofit dedicated to bringing Model UN programs to Title I schools in the DC area, giving students the opportunity to develop leadership, diplomacy, and critical thinking skills.

Our Programs

  • Launching Model UN clubs in underserved schools
  • Providing free training, materials, and conference support
  • Mentoring students in leadership and public speaking
